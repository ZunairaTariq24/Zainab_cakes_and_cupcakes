About Zainab Cakes

Zainab Cakes & Cupcakes is a Rawalpindi-based boutique bakery known for handcrafted, elegant confections. We blend artistry with flavor, offering made-to-order cakes for birthdays, engagements, and special occasions. Our mission is to bring joy through detail-oriented baking, premium ingredients, and timeless aesthetic.

Every cake tells a story — custom-designed, pastel-tinted, and freshly baked to perfection. From bento cakes to multi-tiered masterpieces, we are here to sweeten your moments with grace and love.
